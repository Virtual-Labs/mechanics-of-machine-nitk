Tchebichev Straight Line Mechanism
Theory

Tchebichev Mechanism consists of four links as shown in the figure.

OB and AQ are equal and crossed. Keeping the link lengths in the ratio AB:OQ:AQ = 1:2:2.5 allows the mid point of AB to move approximately in a straight line parallel to OQ.


There is also a Tchebichev Lambda Mechanism which converts rotation to approximate straight line motion with approximate constant velocity.

Procedure

The main purpose of this simulation is to understand the motion in Tchebichev Straight Line Mechanism. Change the parameters under the Variables Section in the simulation to see how the location and length of straight line motion changes.
